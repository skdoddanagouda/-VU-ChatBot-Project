AND MORE                  THE DYNAMIC WORLD OF LEGAL STUDIES IN A DIGITAL ECONOMY
The unique 5-year Law Programs at VU blend traditional and emerging legal domains, empowering you to become a change-maker in today's globalized society.                  Delve deep into the intersection of legal studies with        Technology and Design    Sociology and Political Science    Economics    Business Management
You can explore how legal frameworks adapt to protect intellectual property in a digital era, such as safeguarding the rights of content creators on platforms like YouTube or addressing patent disputes.
You can examine how government policies for curbing the spread of online misinformation impact communities and user behavior.
You can navigate the intricacies of financial law, including cryptocurrency regulations and blockchain contracts.
You can advise businesses on legal implications in the digital realm, such as data privacy compliance for multinational corporations.
PRACTICAL, HANDS-ON LEARNING
Unlock real-world legal experience with our Externship Program. Beyond classrooms, you'll work under legal experts' guidance in government, nonprofits, and more.
Every law student at VU completes a minimum of a 20-week externship. Externship opportunities include:
Trial and Appellate Advocates
Judiciary
Legal Regulatory authorities
Legislatures and Parliament
Market Institutions
Law Firms, Companies
Local Self Government
NGOs
Media Organizations
Other bodies, as the university shall stipulate, where law is practiced either in action, dispute resolution, or management
