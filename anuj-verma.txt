Biography
Dr. Anuj Verma is an accomplished academician and marketing professional with over 15 years of
diversified experience spanning industry and academia. He has published over 20 research papers in
reputed Scopus and ABDC-indexed journals on topics such as consumer behaviour, digital marketing, AI
in CRM, and sustainable marketing. Dr. Verma has also authored a book, contributed case studies, and
presented at leading conferences including IIM Indore, Kozhikode, and Nagpur.
He has completed a CSR consultancy project for the GMR Group and received the Young Achiever’s
Award in Management. His previous corporate roles at Mahindra Holidays, ITC, and Make My Trip lend
practical depth to his academic work.
