Why attend the Vidyashilp Summer Program?
The future belongs to individuals who can think across disciplines and apply knowledge to real-
world challenges.
The Vidyashilp Summer Program offers a short but immersive academic experience designed to
help students explore fields of interest before choosing a university program.
Over the course of the program, participants will:
Explore Interdisciplinary Ideas
Work on Collaborative Projects
Interact with Vidyashilp Faculty & Industry Experts
Experience University Life on Campus.
6-Day Journey
Economics & Business
Economic Thinking: Games on public finance and strategic decision-making.
Leadership & Finance: Modules on team building and financial management.
Engineering & AI
Makerspace: Hands-on robotics, 3D design, and sensor integration.
AI Logic: Prototyping automation and troubleshooting smart systems.
Law & Psychology
Legal Simulation: An immersive dive into the Constitution and legal frameworks.
The Mind: Workshops on positive psychology and mental well-being.
Design & Creative Arts
The Zine Lab: Visual storytelling and self-publishing.
Masterclass Series
Sports & Leadership: A high-performance session led by a former Olympian
Creative Performance: A narrative-led workshop focusing on storytelling and
expression.
Global Innovation: Strategic session on industry trends and global impact.
Future-Mapping: A career masterclass on designing interdisciplinary professional
paths.

What to Expect
Interdisciplinary Exploration:
Students learn how ideas connect across fields and how
complex problems require multiple perspectives.
Learning by Doing:
Participants work in small groups to explore ideas, build projects and
present solutions.
Immersive Interactions:
Engaging sessions led by Vidyashilp faculty and industry
experts who bring academic insight and real-world perspectives.
Collaborative Network:
Network and collaborate with students from other institutions.
Career:
Understand how to think about career and college.
Cultural Immersion:
A singing session by a well known singer MD Pallavi
Interaction:  Interact with Facultys.
Experience Campus:  Experience life at Vidyashilp University through immersive campus activities.
Final Showcase
At the end of the program, students present their projects to faculty & peers.
Parents are invited to attend the final showcase.
Students receive a  Certificate of Achievement  from Vidyashilp University.
Who Should Apply?
9-12th Grade students who are:
Curious about different disciplines.
Exploring future career paths.
Interested in project-based learning.
Eager to experience university life.
What Makes the Vidyashilp Summer Program Unique?
Unlike traditional summer camps, this program introduces students to the Vidyashilp approach
to education.
Students experience:
Interdisciplinary thinking.
Applied learning through projects.
Exposure to real-world ideas & careers.
