Bachelor of
Design
The four-year B.Des program blends the principles of  design and communication, fostering both creative expertise and intellectual depth.
The curriculum integrates design thinking with media, communication, marketing, and digital technologies—preparing students for versatile careers across industries. Aligned with global academic standards, the program meets the four-year requirement for Master’s admissions abroad.
Internship projects after the second and third years offer hands-on industry exposure, ensuring graduates are not only skilled designers but also adaptable problem-solvers ready to lead in a dynamic world.
4 Years
B.Des
Why VU for B.Des?            A Future Ready Focus
Vidyashilp University’s B.Des program offers an exclusive focus on blending classic principles of design and Communication with future-oriented approach. This distinctive curriculum equips students for higher education, numerous career pathways in a rapidly changing world.
Application Based Interdisciplinary Learning
With a strong foundation in liberal arts, the program builds multidisciplinary perspectives through rigorous design training and interdisciplinary minors. Minors such as Marketing and Entrepreneurship (School of Business Studies), Psychology (School of Liberal Arts & Design Studies), and Data Science (School of Computational and Data Sciences) are seamlessly integrated with the major through mandatory Integrative Projects—ensuring students understand design in the context of business, human behavior, and technology.
Research-Driven, Practice-Led
The program stands apart for its equal emphasis on academic research and practical mastery. Students with a scholarly inclination or a practice-oriented mindset will find the curriculum intellectually rewarding and industry-relevant—nurturing advanced analytical and creative skills.
Designed for a Global, Digital World
With a strong experiential learning approach and skill development tailored for global citizenship, the program shapes professionals who can engage meaningfully with individuals, communities, and businesses in a digitally connected world. Learning is guided by highly qualified faculty with extensive academic and industry experience, ensuring a world-class education in design and communication.
