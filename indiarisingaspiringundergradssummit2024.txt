India Rising    Aspiring Undergrads Summit 2024
Dec 14, 2024
09:00 AM
Dr. P Dayananda Pai Auditorium at Vidyashilp Academy
Download Brochure
A Unique Learning Experience
Students were captivated by thought-provoking talks from industry stalwarts and academic leaders, offering fresh perspectives on India’s economic rise and new-age careers that align with this upward trajectory. The experience deepened with a powerful panel discussion on higher education in India vs. abroad, encouraging students to leverage our growing economy. Interdisciplinary workshops and contests provided hands-on exposure to undergraduate learning, while top performers were celebrated with the prestigious Trailblazers of Tomorrow and contest awards.
