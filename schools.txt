Schools                       SCHOOLS
The Schools at Vidyashilp University are concentric in engagement and bring in interdisciplinary dependence to create deep domain knowledge for manifold applications. Through a strong faculty pool and industry connect, the programs are customised and crafted to be
“Real World Ready”
and to provide exposure for various industry application contexts.
School of
Engineering &
Technology
Technology is the engine of transformation — at the School of Engineering &
Technology you’ll learn to build that engine. Grounded in solid engineering
fundamentals, enriched with innovation labs, interdisciplinary projects
and real-world applications, you’ll become a creator of tomorrow’s systems,
tools and solutions. From AI and data to sustainable design and smart infrastructure,
you’ll shape what’s next.
Explore the programs
School of
Liberal Arts &
Sciences
The School of Liberal Arts & Sciences empowers you to explore ideas, ask questions,
and make sense of an ever-changing world. With an interdisciplinary curriculum that
spans economics, psychology, sciences and beyond, you’ll develop breadth and depth,
critical thinking and empathy. Learning here extends beyond classroom walls:
you’ll engage with issues, lead change, and craft a path that’s yours.
Think bigger. Lead with insight.
Explore the programs
School of
Design
At the School of Design,
you’ll go beyond aesthetics to
explore design as a tool for problem-solving,
storytelling, and innovation. With specialisation
tracks across Film & Animation, Graphic Design, Interaction Design,
and more, students learn to think critically, design responsibly,
and create with purpose. By blending creativity with strategy, and
empathy with execution, you’ll gain the ability to shape ideas into
experiences that make a lasting impact.
Explore the programs
School of
Business &
Entrepreneurship
The School of Business & Entrepreneurship equips you with both the business
acumen and the entrepreneurial mindset to build, lead and grow in today’s
dynamic world. Whether you’re launching a venture, driving innovation within
a company or stepping into family business, you’ll gain strategic foundations,
experiential exposure and the founder’s spirit to create value and impact.
Turn ideas into action — start your business story here.
Explore the programs
Law School
At the Law School, you’ll step into a world where ideas,
policies and justice intersect. Our curriculum blends
legal theory with practice, interdisciplinary insight
and the realities of a globalised, digital and regulated world.
Through moot courts, clinics, policy labs and real-case exposure,
you’ll become a legal thinker, advocate, and leader ready to shape
the systems of tomorrow. Your future in law starts here.
