About Us

A University Built for the Real World

Vidyashilp University, located in Bengaluru, is a private university established under the Karnataka Private Universities Act, designed to educate the next generation of leaders, innovators, and change-makers.

  • Industry-integrated curriculum developed with global partners
  • World-class faculty with deep research and industry expertise
  • State-of-the-art 15-acre campus in north Bangalore
  • Strong emphasis on entrepreneurship and innovation
  • Ph.D. and research programs in cutting-edge fields
  • Dedicated placement and career support cell
