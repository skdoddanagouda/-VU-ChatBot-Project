School of Engineering & Technology
The School of Engineering and Technology is shaping the future of innovation, developing engineers who seamlessly blend technical mastery with the versatility to lead in a connected world. Our unique curriculum emphasizes applied, experiential learning, integrating cutting-edge areas like Machine Learning, Generative AI, and ethical technology. We empower students to harness the power of  computational science, data science, and advanced AI  to create impactful, real-world solutions that address complex global challenges.

Opportunities and Achievements
AT VU School of Engineering and Technology, learning extends far beyond traditional labs. Our commitment to interdisciplinary, action-oriented experiential learning is central, ensuring you gain invaluable hands-on experience from day one, translating cutting-edge knowledge into tangible solutions for pressing global needs.
Innovative Hubs
We offer a dynamic learning environment with innovative spaces like the Makers Lab (equipped with state-of-the-art tools including a 3D printer) fostering boundless creativity and practical application at the vital intersection of engineering, design, and humanistic inquiry.
Pioneering Research
We are at the forefront of pioneering research and innovation, tackling 21st-century challenges with a forward-looking perspective. Our faculty and students have groundbreaking research published in prestigious venues like Springer and IEEE Xplore.
Student Clubs and Projects
The Robotics Club delves into cutting edge projects like VURA — Vidyashilp University Robotic Automation. Students engage in highly interdisciplinary projects spanning psychology, economics, design, and law, cultivating the holistic mindset essential for groundbreaking engineering solutions.
Global Engagement & Placements
Mandatory internships are streamlined through the university, with leading companies like Microsoft and Reliance as placement destinations, providing crucial pathways to apply your skills in high-impact industry settings both locally and globally.
VURA
Vidyashilp University Robotic Automation
A homegrown learning platform at VU that supports multiple socially relevant robotics projects. Since its inception, VURA has evolved into a springboard for several student-led innovations. The most prominent among them is  MEERA  — Mindful Empathetic Enduring Robotic Assistance — a humanoid designed to support caregiving in elder care and patient recovery.
