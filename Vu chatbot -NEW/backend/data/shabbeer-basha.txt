Biography
Dr. Shabbeer Basha
is an accomplished professional with expertise in computer vision, machine
learning. With a strong academic background and industry experience, he has contributed in building low
compute vision models, neural architecture search. His work focuses on neural network compression,
deep active learning, multitask learning, and domain adaptation.
Passionate about education and innovation, Shabbeer has published research articles in reputed
journals and conferences.
