Biography
Dr. Bijil Prakash, is the Director of Campus Life , Office of Student Affairs. He is one of the Founding Faculties of Vidyashilp University &  also an Assistant Professor  in the School of
Computational and Data Science in Bangalore, boasts a rich academic and research journey spanning
over 14 years. A substantial part of his experience, encompassing almost a decade, was cultivated at
BITS Pilani.
His academic voyage includes the attainment of a Ph.D. from BITS Pilani, an M.Tech. in Modeling &
Simulation, an M.Sc. in Mathematics, and a B.Sc. in Mathematics. As a published researcher, he
specializes in numerical methods, fractional calculus, image processing, and wavelets.
Dr. Bijil has a diverse teaching background, encompassing Mathematics, Statistics, and Operations
research. He actively engages in curriculum design and faculty development initiatives. Furthermore, he
has made noteworthy contributions to sports and cultural activities, and he plays a pivotal role in
managing the Ph.D. admissions, and timetable coordination.
