B.Tech. (Hons.) in Computer Science & Engineering
– AI/ML
The Bachelor of Technology (Hons.) in Computer Science and Engineering (AI/ML) is a four-year program that focuses on developing expertise in Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML).
This undergraduate honours program in AI/ML is designed to equip students with both the theoretical foundations as well as the practical skills needed to become successful professionals in this exciting and most impactful technology of the 21st century. Through a blend of AI, computer science, mathematics, data science, and ethics & philosophy, students learn to build intelligent systems for the future society. The core curriculum balances foundational areas of computer science with basics of AI including Deep Learning, Reinforcement Learning, and Natural Language Processing. In addition, the students have the opportunity to pursue minors in diverse fields such as Economics, Marketing, Psychology, Finance, Design, and Law
4 Years
B Tech. (Hons.) - AI/ML
The program offers flexibility to the students to specialize into different subareas through a well designed bouquet of elective courses. The students can exercise their choice to focus on advanced AI/ML algorithms  including Probabilistic Graphical Models, Bayesian Methods in Machine Learning, Generative AI, Multi-Modal Learning etc., or focus on AI systems development by doing courses on Full Stack Development, Backend Systems and API Development, Big Data Analytics, and Mobile Application Development.
AI and ML are transforming our world — from healthcare and finance to transportation and creative arts. The undergraduate honours program in AI/ML at Vidyashilp University is designed to train students to become future leaders in this important technology. With highly experienced teachers creating a learning ecosystem that balances theoretical understanding with hands-on practical skills, this program offers students an exciting and unique opportunity to launch successful careers in Artificial Intelligence.

Program Overview
Year-1
Acquire a robust foundation in Mathematics and Computer Science by learning topics such as Calculus, Linear Algebra, Data Structures and Programming, analytical problem solving skills etc.
Year-2
Gain a good background in Computer Science by learning topics such as Computer Organization, Algorithms and advanced programmig concepts. Get a good understanding of the field by learning  basics of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning,  statistical methods, and Data Analysis techniques.
Year-3
Dive deeper into AI/ ML by studying topics such as Deep Learning, Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing, ML System Development, Cloud Computing etc.
Year-4
Acquire good technical competencies through advanced elective courses, Capstone Project and industry internships.

The Evolution of Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning are transforming our world — from healthcare and
finance to transportation and creative arts. The undergraduate honors program in AI/ML at
Vidyashilp University is designed to equip students to be the next generation of innovators and
leaders in this transformative field. Our skilled & experienced faculty help create a learning
ecosystem that balances theoretical understanding with hands-on practical skills, offering
students a unique and exciting opportunity to launch successful careers in Artificial Intelligence.
The global AI market is projected to reach significant value in the coming years, with a
substantial contribution to the worldwide economy, cementing AI/ML Specialists as one of the
fastest-growing and most in-demand job roles globally.
Choose from a diverse range of Elective Courses
The program offers flexibility to the students to delve deep into different subareas through a well
designed bouquet of elective courses. The students can exercise their personal choice to focus
on, e.g., advanced AI/ML methods such as Probabilistic Graphical Models, Bayesian Methods in
Machine Learning, Generative AI, Multi-Modal Learning, or focus on AI systems development
by doing courses on Full Stack Development, Backend Systems and API Development, Big Data
Analytics, or Mobile Application Development.
Why VU for B.Tech.?            Nurturing Future-Ready AI/ML Wizards
The B.Tech. (Hons.) in Computer Science & Engineering (AI/ML) Program at Vidyashilp
University is built to prepare students for an evolving tech-driven world. With a curriculum
that blends core Computer Science and Engineering with advanced AI/ML topics, students
gain the skills to lead in fields like Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Deep

real impact through technology.
Customisable Learning Pathways
Students have the freedom to personalize their academic journey through a range of Elective
Courses for a focussed approach. Interdisciplinary minors in areas like Psychology, Design,
Marketing, Finance, Law, and Economics further expand career possibilities, enabling
students to operate at the intersection of data and domain knowledge. This open academic
system fosters unique combinations of skills tailored to individual ambitions.
Learning Beyond the Classroom
At Vidyashilp University, learning goes beyond lectures. With hands-on projects, internships,
and practical lab sessions, students gain real-world experience that mirrors industry
expectations. This experiential learning model enhances problem-solving, critical thinking,
and technical dexterity — qualities that employers actively seek.
Mentorship & Modern Infrastructure
Students benefit from mentorship by leading academicians and industry experts who bring real-
world insights into the classroom. Coupled with access to cutting-edge infrastructure and
research facilities, the program also emphasizes soft skills like communication, teamwork, and
ethical reasoning — ensuring well-rounded, globally aware graduates who are ready to lead
innovation.

The Expansive Scope of AI/ML: A Career Perspective
The need for skilled AI and ML professionals is experiencing an explosive surge, as businesses
across every industry adopt intelligent systems to automate processes, enhance decision-
making, and create smarter products. This translates into exceptional career prospects and a
dynamic job market.
In-Demand Career Paths & Roles
The program prepares students for a diverse range of specialized and high-value roles that are
currently at the forefront of technological innovation:     Machine Learning Engineer
Focuses on designing, building, and deploying ML models and
platforms. This is consistently cited as one of the most sought-after AI jobs.
Data Scientist
Uses AI-driven techniques, statistical models, and programming to analyze
massive datasets, extract actionable insights, and solve complex real-world problems.
AI Engineer
Develops and implements AI systems and applications that solve real-world
problems, helping organizations increase efficiency and productivity.
Computer Vision and Natural Language Processing (NLP) Specialist
Experts who create
systems to interpret and understand visual data and human language.
AI Research Scientist
Explores new, cutting-edge AI models and algorithms, often working to
push the boundaries of technology in R&D labs.
IoT Developer
Designs, develops, and maintains embedded systems and device-to-cloud
communication for Internet of Things applications.
Cloud Systems Engineer
Designs, implements, and manages scalable, secure, and highly-
available infrastructure on cloud platforms (e.g., AWS, Azure, GCP).
Full Stack Developer
Builds and maintains both the client-side (frontend) and server-side
(backend) of web applications.
DevOps Engineer
Streamlines the software development lifecycle by implementing CI/CD
pipelines, automation, and infrastructure-as-code (IaC).
Business Analyst (BA)
Bridges the gap between business needs and technical solutions by
gathering, documenting, and analyzing requirements from stakeholders.
Product Manager (PM)
Defines the product vision, strategy, and roadmap, prioritizing features
and leading cross-functional teams to deliver value to customers.
Database Engineer
Designs, implements, and maintains database systems to ensure optimal
performance, integrity, and security of stored data.
Internships
Experiential learning is at the heart of the B.Tech. (Hons.) in Computer Science & Engineering
(AI/ML) program at Vidyashilp University. The curriculum is designed to bridge theory and
practice through internships, industry-led projects, and interdisciplinary collaboration. Students
are required to undertake internships that offer hands-on exposure to real-world challenges,
allowing them to sharpen their analytical thinking and adapt to professional environments.
The University partners with industrial and research organizations to provide these invaluable opportunities.
In addition to internships, students engage in interdisciplinary
projects that span psychology, economics, design, marketing, finance, and law—enhancing their
ability to integrate technical skills with domain-specific knowledge. These experiences
collectively prepare students to tackle complex, real-world problems with agility, insight, and
