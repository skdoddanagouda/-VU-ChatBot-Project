Who We Are
IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) is a global network committed to promoting
women in engineering and science, while inspiring young women worldwide to
pursue academic interests leading to careers in these domains. The IEEE WIE
VU Chapter serves as a platform to foster professional growth, networking,
and innovation among students and faculty at Vidyashilp University.
What We Do
The WIE VU Chapter will organize events, workshops, mentorship programs, and collaborative research initiatives to empower women in engineering. Our focus is on knowledge sharing, leadership development, and promoting gender diversity in STEM fields.

Research Initiatives
The IEEE WIE VU Chapter is involved in various research areas, including AI & ML for social good, sustainable engineering solutions, and innovative STEM education methodologies. Our members actively publish research papers and participate in interdisciplinary projects that drive technological advancements.
Events/Workshops/Seminars
The IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) Student Chapter (SBA60216867) was officially launched at Vidyashilp University in the presence of Vice Chancellor Prof. P. G. Babu and Program Chair Prof. Smitha Rao on 7th March 2025. The event was a significant milestone in promoting gender diversity in engineering and technology. Both dignitaries motivated the students with inspiring words, emphasizing the importance of leadership and innovation. The celebration concluded with a cake-cutting ceremony, marking the beginning of an exciting journey for WIE at VU.
