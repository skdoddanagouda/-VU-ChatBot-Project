Job Description
Vidyashilp University is a space for thinking and inquiry Across design, technology, and Humanities    About the Institution:    The School of Design, Vidyashilp University, is an emerging professional institution and an equal opportunity employer within the Vidyashilp Education Group, which has over four decades of academic excellence.   Learn more about us  https://vidyashilp.edu.in/School_of_Design/    Role Expectations:    In addition to teaching and mentoring students, the positions require active contribution to the School’s academic and administrative responsibilities, including programme development, institutional initiatives, consultancies, and research or industry-led projects.    Faculty Positions Open  (All Levels) We invite applications for Assistant Professor, Associate Professor, & Professor in the following broad areas:     AR/VR & Sonic Design -   Teach, lead, and develop project-driven courses and real-time studios in AR, VR, XR, and Sonic Design. Integrate sound, space, interaction, and emerging technologies to create immersive learning and research experiences that foster innovation and drive meaningful outcomes.    Visual Communication Design, Branding & UI/UX -   Teach, mentor, and lead studio-based, project-driven courses in Visual Communication Design, Branding, and UI/UX at undergraduate and postgraduate levels. The role integrates visual systems, brand strategy, and user-centred digital experiences with academic rigour and contemporary practice.    Industrial Design -   Teach, mentor, and lead project-driven Industrial Design studios integrating materials, technology, and design process management, emphasising real-world practice and interdisciplinary collaboration.    Film, Media & Game Design -   Teach and lead project-driven studios in film, media, and game design, integrating narrative, visual language, sound, technology, and interactive systems with hands-on production and critical media thinking.    Technology, New Media & Generative AI -   Teach and lead project-driven studios and courses in emerging technologies, new media, and generative AI, integrating creative coding, computational thinking, and ethical AI practice.     What We Offer     Transparent compensation competitive with leading private universities   Strong institutional support for research and innovation including seed grants for new faculty   A collaborative academic environment within a growing university with a long-term vision   Opportunities to shape academic programs, pedagogy, and research culture from the ground up    Preferred Qualification:    PhD or Master’s degree from reputed design institutions such as IIT/IDC, NID, FTII, or institutes of eminence abroad, with substantial experience in design education, academic administration, and project management across academia and industry.    Age Limit:     Assistant Professor / Associate Professor: Maximum age below 45 years    Professor: Maximum age below 55 years     How to Apply Interested candidates  may send their CV to  Srikanth.bs@vidyashilp.com    Applications Submission Deadline : February 15, 2026. Applications may be accepted after the deadline, subject to availability.  Regards,  HR Team  Vidyashilp University
