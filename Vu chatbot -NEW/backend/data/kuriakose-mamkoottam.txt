Biography
Kuriakose has been engaged in teaching and research during the past 40 years
primarily in the area of
Human Resource Management, Industrial Relations and assessing Living Wage Benchmarks. He has
completed a number of Living Wage Benchmark Studies, several research projects on labour
and industrial relations. He was a visiting fellow (1989-1990) to ESADE Business School,
(Barcelona). He has carried out several consulting assignments and executive development
programmes for Government of India, the ILO and for domestic and multinational
corporations.
He has held administrative and academic leadership positions such as Dean, Faculty of
Management Studies, University of Delhi, Dean of Students, Ambedkar University Delhi,
Executive Chairperson, AUD Centre for Innovation and Incubation, Non-executive member
on the Board of companies, Member on the Governing Council of Colleges, University of
Delhi.
