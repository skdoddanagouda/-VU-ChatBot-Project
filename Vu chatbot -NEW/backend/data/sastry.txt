Biography
P S Sastry obtained B.Sc(Hons) in Physics from IIT, Kharagpur, and BE & PhD from IISc,
Bangalore. He joined the faculty of the department of Electrical Engineering in 1986 and
retired as a professor in 2024. Currently he is an Honorary Professor at the Department of
Electrical Engineering, IISc, Bangalore. He held the Chairmanship of the Department of
Electrical Engineering and ABB Chair Professor at IISc. He held visiting positions at University
of Massachusetts, Amherst; University of Michigan, Ann Arbor; General Motors Research
Laboratories, Warren; Texas A&M University, College Station, USA. His research interests
include Pattern Recognition, Machine Learning, Neural Networks, Pattern Recognition, Data
Mining and Computational Neuroscience. He received C.V. Raman Award for Young
Scientists from Government of Karnataka, Hari Om Ashram Dr. Vikram Sarabhai Research
Award from PRL, Ahmadabad, Most Valued Colleague Award from General Motors
Corporation, and Alumni Award for Excellence in Research from Indian Institute of Science.
He is a Fellow of the Indian National Academy of Engineering and the National Academy of
Sciences, India.
