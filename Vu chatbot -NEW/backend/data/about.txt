Vidyashilp Today
Vidyashilp Education Group has a heritage of nearly four decades in K-12 education. From a single room with 30 students in 1982, the group has grown to 3 schools and 2 preschools in various parts of Bangalore. The Vidyashilp institutions provide an educational experience of the highest quality and aim to advance the frontiers of knowledge and to prepare students for life, work, and leadership.
The Foundation of
Vidyashilp University
Vidyashilp University is the culmination of an outstanding legacy of the Vidyashilp Education Group, committed to excellence in education. Vidyashilp University aims to foster an environment and culture of collaboration, responsiveness, high performance and service. We are people-centred and provide a unique Vidyashilp Experience to our students and staff, focused on nurturing students who will become self-aware, confident, empathetic and resourceful, and leaders who serve the society.

The Foundation of
Vidyashilp University
Vidyashilp University is the culmination of an outstanding legacy of the Vidyashilp Education Group, committed to excellence in education. Vidyashilp University aims to foster an environment and culture of collaboration, responsiveness, high performance and service. We are people-centred and strongly desire to provide a unique Vidyashilp experience to our students and staff, focused on nurturing students who will become self-aware, confident, empathetic and resourceful, and leaders who serve the society.
OUR VISION
We aspire to provide meaningful, collective learning experiences that result in
conscientious leadership for a progressive, yet sustainable society.
OUR VALUES
Integrity
We embrace integrity as a central value in all aspects of personal and professional engagement – including learning, teaching, research, governance and service – to instill a culture of trust and accountability.
Academic
Freedom
We affirm the principles of academic freedom, its rights and responsibilities to advance knowledge through research and creativity, and to educate students to develop their own independence of mind.
Diversity, Equity
and Inclusion
We assert that diversity and interplay of differences is essential to educational excellence and to enrich the individual and collective learning experiences.
Intellectual
Discovery
We believe in the transformative power of curiosity, imagination, learning and teaching, and commit ourselves to creating lifelong learners.
Excellence
We assume ownership of and responsibility for excelling in academic inquiry, scholarship, research, interdisciplinary collaboration and professional growth.
Empathy, Empowerment
and Social Change
We deploy the potential and resilience of our students and all members of the University community to catalyze social change and empathetic upliftment of the underprivileged.          LEADERSHIP  TEAM

Dr. P Dayananda Pai
Chancellor
Vidyashilp University
Dr. P Dayananda Pai is a prominent educationist, philanthropist and leading developer in Bengaluru. Apart from being the Managing Trustee of VSEG, Dr. Pai is also the Board Trustee of B.M.S. College as well as Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan, and a Trustee of Karnataka Chitrakala Parishad. He is also the syndicate member of Manipal University and the National Institute of Technology, Surathkal. He has served as the former syndicate member of Mangalore University. Dr. Pai has been awarded the Karnataka Udyog Award, the Karnataka Rajyotsava Award and the Aryabhatta Award for initiatives involving social and charitable activities. He was conferred an Honorary Doctorate by Mangalore University for his exceptional contribution in the field of education and philanthropy.

Dr. Kiran Pai
Pro-Chancellor
Vidyashilp University
Dr. Kiran Pai (Ed.D.) is the co-founder and Pro-Chancellor of Vidyashilp University. She is also the Director of three prominent schools in Bangalore, India - Vidyashilp Academy, Vidyashilp School and Vidyasagar Preschool.
Dr. Pai is a trained Educational Psychologist with expertise in Instructional Design. She earned her Doctorate in Education (Ed.D) in Organizational Change and Leadership and her Masters in Education in Learning Design and Technology (M.Ed LDT) at the Rossier School of Education, University of Southern California, USA. She was recently awarded the prestigious 2021 Doctoral Distinction Award in recognition of her scholarly work. She is also the recipient of the 2020 Honors from the U.S. based Kappa Delta Pi International Society in Education, which acknowledges only the top 20 percent of prominent international scholars in the field. She was the recipient of the 50 Innovative Leaders award in India awarded by the World Education Congress and Prof. Indira Parikh. Dr Pai undertook most of her professional training in educational leadership at Harvard University, Boston and the IIM-A. Dr. Pai is a member of the American Education Research Association (AERA) and the American Psychological Association (APA).
Under her stewardship as the Director of schools in the last fourteen years, Vidyashilp Academy and Vidyasagar Preschools have won the IBCI School Brilliance awards, World Education awards for Pedagogy, and have been consistently ranked as one of the best co-ed day schools in the country. Additionally, her school’s revolutionary pedagogy has attracted long-term global and national partnerships with organizations such as UNESCO, IISc, IIM-A, University of Missouri, National Institute of Advanced Sciences and the Atal Innovation Mission. Specifically, Vidyashilp Academy is the only school in India identified by UNESCO Asia Pacific for its leading-edge pedagogical practices. To date, numerous organizations and conference papers continue to cite the school as a concrete example of best practices in promoting learner well-being. Additionally, she is also the Founder-Director of Vidyashilp Community Trust, a large-scale government school reform project for over 3,000 disadvantaged youth.

Prof. P.G. Babu
Vice-Chancellor,
Vidyashilp University, Bangalore
A highly experienced academic and administrator, Prof. P.G. Babu served for over
three decades as a Professor at the Indira Gandhi Institute of Development
Research (IGIDR), Mumbai, an institution established by the Reserve Bank of India,
where he was also the Dean of Graduate Studies. Prior to his current role at
Vidyashilp University, he was the Director of the Madras Institute of Development
Studies (MIDS) from 2019 to 2022.
His research and teaching interests are deeply interdisciplinary, focusing on
Microeconomic theory and Game theory with applications across fields such as
Environmental and Natural Resource Economics, Law and Economics, and Politics-
Philosophy and Economics. His significant research contributions include work on
the decision-making framework for climate change as part of the IPCC working
group, which earned him a citation from the Prime Minister of India and was
associated with the Nobel Memorial Peace Prize in 2007.
Globally recognized, Professor Babu held the prestigious Adam Smith Chair
Professorship in Philosophy and Economics at the University of Bayreuth, Germany,
for multiple summer semesters. He has been the Mumbai Coordinator for the
Erasmus Plus European Masters and Doctoral Programs in Law and Economics
since 2009. Additionally, he has held prominent editorial positions, currently serving
as the Managing Editor of the Journal of Quantitative Economics (Springer Nature)
and previously as the Editor-in-Chief of the Review of Development and
Change (Sage). An alumnus of the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), where he
earned his Ph.D., Professor Babu is also a member of the Board of Governors of the
Institute of Economic Growth (IEG) Delhi.
