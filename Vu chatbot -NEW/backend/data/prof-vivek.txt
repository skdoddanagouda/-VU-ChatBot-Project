Biography
Dr. Vivek N.D. is a researcher and public policy analyst specialising in global health governance and
international relations in which his Ph.D thesis is situated. He has had fifteen years of experience leading
interdisciplinary teams across academia, government and international organisations. His publications
span peer-reviewed journals, think tank reports and op-eds in various national and international media.
Trained as a political scientist, he specialises in global health governance, development issues and non-
traditional areas of international relations in relation to Asia and Africa. He has taught courses in political
science, international relations and public policy at the undergraduate and postgraduate levels. He was
the Geneva Health Files Global Fellow 2024.
