Biography
Prabhakaran is a seasoned marketing and communications professional with an experience of over 24 years.
He has worked with some of the biggest brands across different categories, including FMCG, Tech, Retail,
Travel & Aviation, and Media. He has conceptualized and executed award-winning campaigns for Coca-Cola,
Intel, ITC and VMWare which helped these brands to engage with their customers
and establish their leadership position in the market.
Prabhakaran takes a keen interest in teaching and has been doing so for close to 15 years. He has taught various marketing-related
subjects like Marketing Strategy, Integrated Marketing Communications, and Retail Management.
He is a certified case method faculty from Harvard Business School.
Prabhakaran holds an MBA from Steinbeis University, Germany.
He is currently the Vice President at Avian WE, a global PR consultancy firm.
