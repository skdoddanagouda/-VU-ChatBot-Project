(Hons. with Research) - with a focus on Digital Business
The Bachelor of Management Studies (Hons.) with Research or BMS (Hons.) with Research Program at Vidyashilp University is a versatile degree that prepares the student for a wide variety of career pathways in business. The BMS (Hons.) with Research program from the School of Business Studies provides the essential components of a traditional Business degree plus a broad selection of cutting-edge technology and advanced Digital Business Management and marketing tools. This digital theme is introduced in multiple courses to prepare students for success in a global economy where every industry is being transformed by disruptive new technologies and needs to be reimagined for the Digital Economy.
The Undergraduate program in Management Studies builds the foundation necessary to pursue business-oriented entry roles in various domains of Business and Management and to pursue academic research if one so chooses.  On completion of the BMS (Hons) with Research program, Vidyashilp University graduates
have the advantage of a direct admission to a Ph.D program as per the NEP.

Digital Business
Digital business is transforming the way organizations use
and perceive technology, shifting it from a supporting role to a leading force
in innovation, revenue generation, and market growth. On the path to a digital
future, companies require not only the courage and determination to revamp
processes but also qualified professionals who can introduce new ideas and
drive emerging business domains forward.
The future of business is undeniably digital. The key is to
educate future business leaders who understand the opportunities and challenges
of Industry 4.0 and Digital Business, make informed decisions, and lead digital
transformation within their organizations. To prepare future managers for these
challenges, the School of Business Studies offers a curriculum focused on Digital
Business.
The rigorous curriculum takes an interdisciplinary approach
to business fundamentals, enabling students to transition seamlessly from understanding
"what is" to envisioning "what will be." It integrates
diverse perspectives, fosters critical thinking, and reinforces growing
knowledge through experiential learning opportunities.              Why VU for BMS?            A unique General Management program
This combines digital business orientation with a wide choice of minor fields like Data
Science, Economics, Psychology and  Design.
Success through a different lens
Through behavioural assessment conducted in the University, the University's mentoring systems, and the extra focus made on the Communication and Leadership Labs at the University, the graduates would view success with a different lens.

Sample Careers
Exciting career paths await in businesses, corporates,
public and private sectors, government, non-profits, law, international
relations, and academia, allowing you to make a meaningful impact wherever your
passion lies.
Leadership roles in management—whether in Operations,
Project Management, Human Resources, or Finance—offer opportunities to grow,
lead teams, and drive change.
For aspiring entrepreneurs, the chance to build and scale
your own business has never been more promising. With the right skills and
mindset, you can turn ideas into reality.
If you enjoy data and insights, roles like Market Analyst,
Product Analyst, and Research Analyst let you dive deep into trends and shape
business strategies.
Further studies—whether an MBA, PG Diploma, or professional
certification (CA, CS, etc.) at top B-schools in India and abroad—open doors to
specialization and career advancement.
Industry Ready
Read more on well-known examples of Digital Business in the real world
Read More
Uber capitalizes on the widespread adoption of smartphones and utilizes a business model that allows them to maintain relatively low capital assets. A traditional taxi company must own and maintain vehicles, as well as contribute overhead to recruiting and managing employees; an e-business version would allow customers to schedule a taxi online and perhaps view past trips, but the essential experience would remain the same. Uber cements its place as a digital business because it is, at its core, a platform that connects people and drivers at a massive scale through the internet, while creating an experience that improves on what was previously available to people.
Read More
Netflix is a good example of a company that switched from e-business to digital business. Originally, Netflix used technology to manage an inventory system and mail people DVDs, providing an experience that was more convenient, but ultimately fairly similar to movie rental stores. However, once it gave customers the option to stream video on demand, it disrupted itself and transformed the way that people view film and television media. The ability to consume movies and TV shows at any time or place is impossible without the widespread internet access that most consumers have today.
Read More
Disney is one traditional business that has embraced digital technology in order to enhance experiences in its theme parks. The goal of its MagicBand (integrated with a guest’s My Disney Experience account), a wristband that uses RFID and radio to connect with sensors in the park, is to improve five key elements that are part of every Park experience: going to an attraction, staying in a hotel, dining at a restaurant, taking a photo and sending it to friends or family, and buying merchandise as a souvenir. Guests receive the MagicBand a few weeks before the visit and can use it to enter the park, buy food or merchandise, reserve attractions, and watch real time data on wait times. At the end of the day, guests have an experience that moves fluidly between digital and physical. (Disney has announced that beginning in January 2021, it plans to launch new offerings as part of the My Disney Experience App that bring the features of a MagicBand to guests’ smart devices.)
Internships
The Summer Internship is a 6 to 8-week immersive experience
designed to provide hands-on learning in a business organization, industry, or
social enterprise. As an on-the-job intern, students gain real-world exposure,
apply classroom concepts in dynamic work environments, and develop critical
professional skills.
Internship opportunities span a wide range of industries
including consulting, web commerce, IT, technology, manufacturing, hospitality,
media, and more. Many leading organizations actively seek future-ready
graduates, offering internships at Amazon, Ernst & Young, Deloitte,
Flipkart, Airtel, Reliance, etc. and fast-growing new-age businesses such as Swiggy,
Zomato, Meesho, Razorpay, CRED, Zepto, and Dunzo—all known for their innovation
and industry disruption.
The Capstone Project/Internship is an opportunity to deep
dive into a chosen domain, whether within an industry, business organization,
or at the university. The goal is to gain practical insights, problem-solving
experience, and a strategic understanding of real-world challenges, equipping
students with a competitive edge in their careers.
