Biography
Ankita Tripathi is an accomplished academician and researcher with over eight years of
experience in teaching and research in the field of accounting and finance. She holds a Ph.D.
in Accounting and Finance and has a strong foundation with an M. Com in the same field.
She has taught a wide range of accounting and finance subjects to undergraduate and
postgraduate students, including courses integrated with professional programs such as CA,
ACCA, and CIMA. Alongside teaching, she has actively contributed to student engagement
and institutional initiatives through various academic coordination roles.
She also worked as a Research Associate at Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS)
Pilani on an ICSSR-sponsored project. Her research interests include financial inclusion,
MSME efficiency, working capital management, sustainable finance, and fintech. She has
published extensively in Scopus- and ABDC-listed journals, contributed book chapters with
international publishers such as Springer and Taylor & Francis, and presented her work at
renowned national and international conferences, including at IIM Jammu and the
University of Cambridge, UK.
With practical industry exposure through Chartered Accountancy training, she aims to
bridge the gap between theory and practice and inspire future finance professionals
through quality teaching and impactful research.
