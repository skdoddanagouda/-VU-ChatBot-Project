OPPORTUNITIES AND ACHIEVEMENTS
Legal Awareness Programme
Legal Awareness Programmes – students conduct session at government schools on Fundamental Rights and Duties, with an aim to create legal awareness.
Global Recognition
Learn from internationally acclaimed faculty while gaining exposure to global platforms. Students like Saniya Khan have represented the university at prestigious events, including the  International Mediation Clinic Network Conference in Glasgow .
Moot Court Simulations
Moot Court simulations go beyond the classroom – you will hone your advocacy and negotiation skills in realistic scenarios, including mock board meetings and corporate dispute resolutions. These high-intensity sessions sharpen legal reasoning, build courtroom confidence, and prepare you to navigate the complexities of real-world litigation and arbitration.
Externships
Gain practical exposure through real cases and client interactions in compliance, regulation, and corporate law. Students have interned with leading firms and organizations such as King Stubb & Kasiva, MT Nanaiah & Associates, and more, applying their learning to real-world challenges.
Centre for Market Regulations and Governance (CMRG)
A pioneering hub for interdisciplinary research at the intersection of law, economics, and governance. CMRG drives thought leadership and collaboration between academia, industry, and government to strengthen regulatory frameworks, enhance institutional capacity, and promote effective policy-making.
Legal Clinic
Make a tangible difference in society by offering your legal skills to those in need. You'll actively engage with legal and local bodies, including enriching visits to the Karnataka Mediation Centre, extending your learning directly into the heart of the legal system.
