Biography
As an accomplished assistant professor and experienced ETL developer, Jyothsna has a unique
blend of academic and technical expertise. Having 17.5 years of experience she is skilled in designing
and delivering interactive and stimulating lectures, developing course materials, and assessing student
progress. In this role as an assistant professor, she has developed and delivered challenging and
engaging courses in various subjects, mentored, and advised students, and conducted research that has
contributed to the field.
With 1.5 years of experience as an ETL developer, she has gained expertise in designing, developing,
and implementing ETL solutions, using technologies such as SQL, Python, and ETL tools and gained
experience in data modelling, performance tuning, and troubleshooting. Throughout the career, she has
demonstrated the ability to effectively manage projects, work collaboratively with cross-functional teams,
and communicate complex technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders. As a passionate and
motivated educator and developer, she is committed to use the skills and experience to drive innovation
and excellence in both academic and professional settings.
