B.Tech. (Hons.) Computer Science and Engineering
– (Data Science)
Vidyashilp University's B.Tech. (Hons.) program is a specialization in Data Science built on Computer Science and Engineering. It is a unique combination of interdisciplinary program work designed for those who seek to become 'Data Scientists'. The curriculum design and structure are guided both by recent work on the relation of Data Science to the other domains as well as the need for talent to meet the demands of the data-driven economy of the future. Graduates of the B.Tech. program in Computer Science and Engineering will be uniquely positioned to pursue a career in industry, academia, or research in this rapidly growing and all-pervasive field of Data Science.

DOMAIN EXPERTISE
Data Science
In the past decade, the world has been transformed by the rapidly evolving field of Data Science. This new science, which is already revolutionizing business, science, and society, builds on an array of technological developments, including the widespread use of smartphones and rapid technological progress in computing and communication. Massive investments have gone into building out wireless infrastructure, data centers (the cloud) and into leveraging such facilities. The volume of data being collected automatically—and the processing of such data—has soared. New data-driven services have arisen, exploiting this new data-driven environment and convincing the public of the power and elegance of the data-driven paradigm.
Today, the term 'Data Scientist' typically describes a knowledge worker who uses the complex and massive data resources characteristic of this new era. However, Data Science is a broader concept involving principles for data collection, storage, integration, analysis, inference, communication, and ethics appropriate for this new data-driven era. Several industries and academic disciplines have perceived that a new field of Data Science is emerging out of several established fields, including Information Technology, Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, Operations Management, and Business Analytics. Data Scientists of the future will need to be educated in the full scope of Data Science principles, which is prioritized in the best B.Tech. colleges in Bangalore.              Why VU for B.Tech.?            Nurturing Future-Ready Data Scientists
The B.Tech. (Hons.) in the Data Science program at Vidyashilp University is built to prepare students for an evolving tech-driven world. With a curriculum that blends core Computer Science and Engineering with advanced Data Science topics, students gain the skills to lead in fields like Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning. This future-focused academic approach ensures graduates are equipped to create real impact through technology.
Customizable Learning Pathways
Students have the freedom to personalize their academic journey through specialization tracks such as Applied Deep Learning and Full Stack Development. Interdisciplinary minors in areas like Psychology, Design, Marketing, Finance, Law, and Economics further expand career possibilities, enabling students to operate at the intersection of data and domain knowledge. This open academic system fosters unique combinations of skills tailored to individual ambitions.
Learning Beyond the Classroom
At Vidyashilp University, learning goes beyond lectures. With hands-on projects, internships, and practical lab sessions, students gain real-world experience that mirrors industry expectations. This experiential learning model enhances problem-solving, critical thinking, and technical dexterity — qualities that employers actively seek.
Mentorship and Modern Infrastructure
Students benefit from mentorship by leading academicians and industry experts who bring real-world insights into the classroom. Coupled with access to cutting-edge infrastructure and research facilities, the program also emphasizes soft skills like communication, teamwork, and ethical reasoning — ensuring well-rounded, globally aware graduates who are ready to lead innovation.

Internships
Experiential learning is at the heart of the B.Tech. (Hons.) in Data Science program at Vidyashilp University. The curriculum is designed to bridge theory and practice through internships, industry-led projects, and interdisciplinary collaboration. Students are required to undertake internships that offer hands-on exposure to real-world challenges, allowing them to sharpen their analytical thinking and adapt to professional environments. The University partners with leading organizations such as EvueMe, Cytecare, and others to provide these invaluable opportunities. In addition to internships, students engage in interdisciplinary projects that span psychology, economics, design, marketing, finance, and law—enhancing their ability to integrate technical skills with domain-specific knowledge.
