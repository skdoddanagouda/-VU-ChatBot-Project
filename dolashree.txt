Biography
Dolashree holds an LLM from University College London with a
specialisation in Jurisprudence & Legal Theory. She
has previously worked at RV University, O.P.
Jindal Global University and Azim Premji University.
Her research and writing spans the area of Law & Marginalization,
Law & Society, Gender, Governance and Education.
She has held various administrative positions at Jindal Global Law School
and RV University. She is keen on building learning communities within
university and extending academic support.
